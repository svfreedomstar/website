Boat

Built in 1994 by Catalina Yachts, Freedom Star is a 1995 Catalina 36' MKii. She has a 4'8" draft on a wing keel. She's a bermuda rigged sloop capable of handling winds up to 30kts. She's 31' at the waterline making her a pretty fast ship at 7kts.
She has two (2) Kyocera 130w solar panels.
